## Tier 1 — Malaysian industry directories (1-2 week effort)

### 4. CIDB Malaysia (Construction Industry Development Board)

- **URL:** https://www.cidb.gov.my/
- **Why:** CIDB is the regulatory body for Malaysian construction. A CIDB listing is mandatory anyway for public-sector contracting; the directory listing is a high-trust backlink.
- **Action:** Confirm your CIDB registration is current. If yes, request your company is listed in the directory. If your CIDB grade is G7 (highest), ensure that's reflected on your homepage.

### 5. IEM (The Institution of Engineers, Malaysia)

- **URL:** https://www.myiem.org.my/
- **Why:** IEM is the professional body for Malaysian engineers. Listing under "Geotechnical Engineering" or "Civil Engineering" specialty groups is a trusted citation.
- **Action:** Confirm Dr. Lai's IEM membership. Submit a 1-paragraph profile to the IEM newsletter / member directory.

### 6. CIDB Construction Quality Assessment System (CONQUAS) reference

- **URL:** CONQUAS publishes quality benchmarks. Submit one of your completed walls (Templer Hills, Istana Negara, Padma Bridge Jajira) as a case study reference.

### 7. Master Builders Association Malaysia (MBAM)

- **URL:** https://mbam.org.my/
- **Why:** Trade body for contractors. Member listing is a low-effort high-relevance backlink.

### 8. Malaysian Geotechnical Society (MGS) — Engineering Institute of Malaysia

- **URL:** https://www.myges.org/ (or via IEM)
- **Why:** Geotechnical specialty society. Submit Dr. Lai's published papers (the 3 PDFs in `research/papers/`) for the MGS newsletter / archive.

### 9. JKR (Jabatan Kerja Raya) Vendor Directory

- **URL:** https://www.jkr.gov.my/ (search for "Senarai Kontraktor Berdaftar")
- **Why:** Government-specified retaining wall contractors are listed in the JKR vendor directory. You should already be on it — confirm and ensure your details/profile are current.
