Privacy Policy

AnchorSOL® Wall Sdn. Bhd. (Co. No. 199901015121 / 490021-M) operates anchorsolwall.com. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

Last updated: 15 May 2026.

1. Who we are

AnchorSOL® Wall Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian-incorporated retaining wall contractor with HQ at C27-1 Plaza Jelutong, Persiaran Gerbang Utama, Bukit Jelutong, 40150 Shah Alam, Selangor. We are the data controller for anchorsolwall.com.

Contact for privacy queries: lai@anchorsolwall.com or WhatsApp +60 12-236 2159.

2. Data we collect

Automatically (via cookies and analytics)

  • Google Analytics 4 data: anonymised IP address, browser type, device type, pages viewed, time on page, referring URL. Used to understand site usage and improve content.
  • Cloudflare / GitHub Pages server logs: IP address, timestamp, requested URL, HTTP status, user agent. Standard server logs retained for typical web-hosting durations.

From you directly (when you contact us)

  • WhatsApp messages you send to +60 12-236 2159 (via wa.me links on the site).
  • Email messages you send to lai@anchorsolwall.com or anchorsolwall@gmail.com.
  • Project information you choose to share when enquiring (site location, wall height, project type, contact name, contact details).

We do not use contact forms with sensitive-data collection. We do not collect credit card data or financial account information on the website.

3. How we use your data

  • To respond to your project enquiries with cost bands, programme estimates, and engineering advice.
  • To send tailored catalogues, specifications, or method statements you request.
  • To maintain a record of project communications for ongoing engagements.
  • To improve the website's usability and content depth through aggregated analytics.

We do not sell your data, share it with marketing partners, or use it for targeted advertising on third-party sites.

4. Cookies

anchorsolwall.com uses cookies only for Google Analytics 4 traffic measurement. We use the anonymize_ip setting so individual IP addresses are not stored in identifiable form.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by blocking the relevant cookie / script via your browser settings.

5. Your rights under PDPA

Under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (Malaysia), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate personal data.
  • Withdraw consent to our processing of your data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Lodge a complaint with the Department of Personal Data Protection (JPDP) of the Ministry of Communications.

To exercise any of these rights, email lai@anchorsolwall.com with your request.

6. Data retention

Project enquiry communications are retained for the duration of the project plus 7 years for warranty and legal-compliance purposes, then securely deleted. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics 4 defaults (typically 14 months for event data).

7. Third-party services we use

  • GitHub Pages (web hosting): standard server logs.
  • Google Analytics 4: aggregated traffic measurement.
  • WhatsApp Business (Meta): when you message us via wa.me links. WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies to that platform.
  • Gmail (Google): when you email anchorsolwall@gmail.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy as the website evolves or as Malaysian data protection law changes. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be noted with a banner on the homepage.
