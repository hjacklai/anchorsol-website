Specifying retaining walls for Kedah projects

For specifiers, QSs, and approving authorities writing tenders for Kedah retaining-wall projects:

  • Design standards: BS 8006-1:2010 (primary), FHWA NHI-10-024 (parametric reference), JKR Standard Specification (materials and workmanship). For Kedah, standard peninsular seismic coefficient kh = 0.05 to 0.10.
  • Backfill spec: anchored MSE allows crusher run at ≥34° friction angle. Friction-based MSE requires premium granular at ≥36°. Crusher run from local Malaysian quarries typically meets the anchored spec.
  • Concrete grade: Grade 30 minimum for inland walls, Grade 35 to 40 for coastal or aggressive environments (relevant for Kedah coastal projects).
  • Reinforcement: hot-dip galvanized carbon steel deformed bar with BS 8006 Annex B sacrificial-thickness allowance for the 75 to 120 year design life.
  • Pre-qualification: CIDB G7 minimum, ≥10 named MSE wall projects, professional engineer leading design, ISO 9001 quality system, in-house manufacturing.
