Building walls in Kajang

Kajang and the broader Kajang-Bangi-Semenyih corridor sits on weathered granite saprolite with patches of older alluvial fill in the valley floors. The terrain steps gradually up from KL towards the Negeri Sembilan border, so retaining walls here are mostly hillside-platform creation for new township development. Granular crusher run is locally available from Bangi and Beranang quarries.

Kajang anchors the southern Klang Valley growth corridor along with Bangi, Semenyih, and Cyberjaya. Continuous township roll-out (Setia Eco Hill, Kajang 2, Setia Eco Glades, Eco Majestic Semenyih) drives consistent retaining-wall demand. The MRT1 Sungai Buloh-Kajang Line terminates at Kajang Station and the MRT2 Putrajaya Line crosses south of Kajang town. Education hub: UNITEN, USIM, MMU, UKM.

Local authority: MPKj (Majlis Perbandaran Kajang). Nearby landmarks the local engineering community uses for site reference: Kajang town, Bandar Baru Bangi, Semenyih, Setia Eco Hill, Kajang 2, Setia Eco Glades, Eco Majestic Semenyih, UKM, MMU Cyberjaya (adjacent), Genting Highlands road (south-east connection).

What we build in Kajang

Hillside platform creation for new township development across the Kajang-Semenyih corridor. Highway-interchange walls on ELITE and the SILK expressway. Education-campus boundary and slope walls (UNITEN Bangi, UKM Bangi).

The wall systems available across these applications:

  • Anchored MSE wall, the AnchorSOL system: precast concrete facing panels, hot-dip galvanised tendons, discrete deadman block anchors, crusher-run backfill. Most economical above 5 m wall height. More on MSE wall fundamentals →
  • Reinforced Earth (RE) wall, the steel-strip variant: cruciform precast facing, ribbed steel strip reinforcement, granular sand backfill. Henri Vidal's original 1963 system. More on RE walls →
  • Reinforced Soil (RS) wall, the geosynthetic variant: HDPE geogrid or PET woven grid reinforcement, segmental block or precast facing, crusher-run backfill. Faster to install on tight footprints. More on RS walls →
  • RC retaining wall, conventional reinforced-concrete cantilever or counterfort: for walls under 5 m, or where space, programme, or aesthetic favours RC over MSE. More on RC retaining walls →

FAQ — retaining wall contractor in Kajang

Do you build retaining walls in Kajang?

Yes. AnchorSOL Wall Sdn. Bhd. delivers anchored MSE walls, RE walls, RS walls, and RC retaining walls in Kajang and the wider Selangor state. Local authority is MPKj (Majlis Perbandaran Kajang). Our delivery is via the same precast manufacturing in Bukit Jelutong, Shah Alam, with project-managed haulage to Kajang.

What wall type is best for Kajang ground conditions?

Selection depends on the specific site. Kajang and the broader Kajang-Bangi-Semenyih corridor sits on weathered granite saprolite with patches of older alluvial fill in the valley floors. The terrain steps gradually up from KL towards the Negeri Sembilan border, so retaining walls here are mostly hillside-platform creation for new township development. Granular crusher run is locally available from Bangi and Beranang quarries. Wall-system selection follows: hillside cuts favour anchored MSE; soft-foundation sites need ground improvement before MSE; urban-infill plots may need RC counterfort where space is constrained.

Which Kajang agencies have you worked with?

We deliver to federal agencies (JKR, PLUS, LLM, KTMB) operating in Selangor, plus the local authority MPKj (Majlis Perbandaran Kajang) where municipal walls are specified. Project list available on request.

How does delivery to Kajang work?

Precast facing panels are manufactured at our Bukit Jelutong (Shah Alam) facility, then hauled to Kajang on flat-bed trucks. Tendon and anchor block production is co-located. A 3 to 4 person erection crew mobilises with the panels and completes 30 to 80 m² of wall per day on site.
