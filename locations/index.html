MSE wall in Selangor

Selangor is the centre of AnchorSOL®'s delivered portfolio. The state hosts the company's HQ in Bukit Jelutong, Shah Alam, the precast manufacturing facility, and the largest concentration of projects by m² and by count. Klang Valley expressway corridors, township developments in Cheras, Kajang, Subang, Sungai Buloh and Semenyih, hill stabilisation across Templer Hills and Bukit Antarabangsa, and federal-government compounds in the Klang Valley all sit in this catchment.

Marquee Selangor projects

  • East Klang Valley Expressway (EKVE), LLM concession, 25,000 m²
  • NKVE Jalan Meru Link, PLUS Expressways, 11,000 m²
  • Elmina Business Park 2 Phase 4B, Sime Darby Property, 12,452 m²
  • Mixed Development Semenyih, Duta Villa, 12,000 m²
  • Templer Hills hillside stabilisation
  • Bukit Antarabangsa hillside retention

Selangor cities and townships AnchorSOL® has worked across

Shah Alam, Petaling Jaya, Subang Jaya, Klang, Cheras, Kajang, Semenyih, Sungai Buloh, Bukit Jelutong, Bukit Antarabangsa, Damansara, Rawang, Bandar Saujana Putra, Bandar Utama, Bukit Bintang area suburbs, Selayang, Ulu Klang, Hulu Selangor, Sepang. See all Selangor projects →

MSE wall in Kuala Lumpur (Federal Territory)

Kuala Lumpur is the densest infrastructure market in Malaysia. Federal Territory projects on AnchorSOL®'s book span urban expressway interchanges, sub-track rail embankments, national-monument compounds, drainage and flood works on the Klang and Gombak river systems, and high-rise mixed development boundary walls.

Marquee KL projects

  • Duta-Ulu Klang Expressway (DUKE) Phase 2, Ekovest / LLM, 22,000 m²
  • Sungai Besi-Ulu Kelang Expressway (SUKE) Package CA1, Prolintas, 22,000 m²
  • Setiawangsa-Pantai Expressway (DUKE Phase 3), LLM / Ekovest, 8,200 m²
  • Istana Negara Jalan Duta, the National Palace, JKR, 8,000 m²
  • KL-Putrajaya Highway Packages 3 & 4, LLM / Maju Holdings, 8,000 m²

KL districts where AnchorSOL® has delivered

Jalan Duta, Setiawangsa, Pantai, Cheras, Wangsa Maju, Ampang, Bukit Bintang, KL Sentral approaches, Bangsar, Damansara Heights, Mont Kiara approach roads, Sungai Besi. See all KL projects →

MSE wall in Johor

Johor's marquee programme on the AnchorSOL® book is the Iskandar Regional Development Authority coastal corridor between Johor Bahru and Nusajaya. Marine retention, reclamation walls, township boundary walls, and federal-road retaining works across southern Johor.

Marquee Johor projects

  • Lebuhraya Pesisiran Pantai JB-Nusajaya, Section 3, Iskandar Regional Development Authority, 23,000 m²
  • Coastal reclamation walls along the JB-Nusajaya corridor
  • Township boundary walls in Iskandar Puteri, Pasir Gudang, Senai, Kulai approach roads

Johor districts AnchorSOL® has delivered in

Johor Bahru, Iskandar Puteri (Nusajaya), Pasir Gudang, Senai, Kulai, Kluang approach roads, Batu Pahat federal-road sections, Pontian coastal works, Mersing approach roads. See all Johor projects →

MSE wall in Pahang

Pahang is Malaysia's largest state by area on the Peninsular and AnchorSOL®'s marquee Pahang programme is the Kuantan Port City reclamation and waterfront retention. Port retaining structures, marine-spec walls, and East Coast Economic Region Development infrastructure across the Kuantan corridor.

Marquee Pahang projects

  • Kuantan Port City, East Coast Economic Region Development (ECERD), 14,500 m²
  • Sungai Pahang flood-mitigation walls (JPS)
  • Federal-road retaining walls on the East Coast Highway and Karak Highway approaches

Pahang districts where AnchorSOL® has worked

Kuantan, Pekan, Temerloh, Bentong, Raub, Cameron Highlands approach roads, Maran. See all Pahang projects →

Marquee Perak projects

  • State-road retaining works across Taiping, Manjung and Kampar approach roads

Perak districts AnchorSOL® has delivered in

MSE wall in Sabah

AnchorSOL® delivers Sabah projects via container freight to Kota Kinabalu, Sandakan and Tawau, with the local crew trained for East Malaysia mobilisation.

Sabah project context
